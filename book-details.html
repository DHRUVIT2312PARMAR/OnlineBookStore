About the Author

Alex Johnson is a renowned AI researcher and practitioner with over 15 years of experience in artificial intelligence and machine learning.

Biography

Dr. Alex Johnson holds a Ph.D. in Computer Science from Stanford University and has been at the forefront of AI research for more than a decade. He has published numerous papers on autonomous systems and intelligent agents, and his work has been featured in leading technology publications.

Before becoming a full-time author and consultant, Alex worked as a senior AI engineer at several Silicon Valley tech companies, where he led teams developing cutting-edge AI solutions for various industries including healthcare, finance, and autonomous vehicles.
